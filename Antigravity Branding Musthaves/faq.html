Process
How does a project start?

Every project starts with an intake form. This maps out your brand, audience, goals, and wishes. Prefer a call? That works too. We always start with a clear picture of what you need.

How long does a project take?

For Full Custom Design and Scan & Upgrade we plan 4 to 6 weeks. For Template Customization that is 1 to 2 weeks. The timeline depends on how quickly you provide feedback.

How many feedback rounds are included?

Full Custom Design and Scan & Upgrade include 3 feedback rounds. Template Customization includes 2. That gives you enough room to get the design exactly right.

What if I don't know which service I need?

No problem. Send a message via the contact page and describe your situation as best you can. We'll help you choose the option that best fits your stage and budget.

Pricing
What are the costs?

Scan & Upgrade and Full Custom Design start from €2,000. Template Customization starts from €950. The exact price depends on the scope and complexity of your project.

Are there hidden costs?

No. Before we start you receive a clear proposal with scope, price, and timeline. No surprises afterward.

Are there payment terms?

Yes. We work with a deposit at the start and the remaining amount upon delivery. The exact split is stated in the proposal.

Services
What is the difference between Custom Design and Template Customization?

With Full Custom Design we build your website entirely from scratch, fully tailored to your brand. With Template Customization we choose one of our high-end templates together and customise it with your branding, copy, and imagery. Both result in a professional, unique website.

What exactly is Scan & Upgrade?

We start with a thorough scan of your current website. We map out the weak spots and use them as the blueprint for a completely new digital foundation. The result is a brand new website, not a patch on the existing one.

Do you also provide copy and images?

We focus on design and technical realisation. You provide the copy and images yourself. Need help with this? We can refer you to trusted copywriters and photographers.

After Launch
Do you offer maintenance after launch?

Yes. We offer packages for ongoing maintenance and minor adjustments. That way your website always stays up-to-date and well maintained.

Can I edit the website myself after delivery?

That depends on the platform the site is built on. Upon delivery we always give you a brief instruction so you can make simple edits yourself.

What if I want to change something after launch?

Small adjustments right after launch we discuss case by case. For larger changes we draw up a separate proposal. We always think along with you.
